Shirt Factory is an apparel company based on Portland, Oregon. We develop ways to use 21st century communications technology to get the design from the mind of the customer to the printer and back to the customer as fast and easily as possible.

Shirt Factory website and app

Basic functionality

  • Tshirt generators that make shirts with user-given parameters
  • Users can make their own design
  • Marketplace lets users easily upload and sell their design

Development steps

  1. Make functional website with working generators - ex: ASCII art
    • Back end uses Python
  2. Develop user-designed shirt interface
  3. Port system to app for iOS and Android
  4. Develop marketplace with parameters that make sense given Etsy
